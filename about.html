About

Career

I've spent 16.5 years in electronic component distribution. I started my career in products as a Product Assistant, entering information and assisting the Product Managers on the Semiconductor and Opto-embedded teams with tasks.
I then moved to a team that was a bit of the jack of all trades to all Product Managers.
After that, I moved to Marketing where I helped to build microsites focusing on individual parts for promotional purposes.
My last job on the Products floor was in Asset Management. I coordinated with the Product Managers, suppliers, and the warehouse to close out issues with stock returns, incorrect shipments, and stock rotations and scrapping of product.
I was then recruited to help on the Bill of Materials team, quoting large volume quotes on the Sales floor.
Through the years I became the most productive member of the team and was promoted three times, eventually becoming the team Coordinator.
I then left that company to build a team doing similar work.
